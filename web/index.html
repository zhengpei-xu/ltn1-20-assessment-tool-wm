CLoS Score
— Overall
Safety
—

Weight: 8/25 of overall CLoS

Evaluates traffic risk to cyclists using 5 of 8 LTN 1/20 criteria: operating speed (V85), traffic volume (AADT), HGV proportion, collision history (KSI density), and kerbside activity. Each link is scored 0–2 per criterion; the route score is a length-weighted average across all links.

Attractiveness
—

Weight: 5/25 of overall CLoS

Measures how pleasant and inviting a route feels, using 3 of 5 LTN 1/20 criteria: street lighting provision, effective cycling width, and social safety (overlooking, passive surveillance). Length-weighted average across route links.

Comfort
—

Weight: 4/25 of overall CLoS

Assesses surface quality and ride comfort using 1 of 4 LTN 1/20 criteria (A19: surface condition of cycle facilities). Only scored on links with dedicated cycle provision — if the route has no facilities, comfort is excluded and the remaining dimensions are re-weighted.

Directness
—

Weight: 5/25 of overall CLoS

A route-level metric using 2 of 5 LTN 1/20 criteria: detour ratio (straight-line distance ÷ actual route distance; >0.8 = good) and gradient assessment against LTN 1/20 Table 5-8 (maximum continuous climb lengths by gradient %). Unlike other dimensions, this is not a link-level average but a property of the whole route geometry.

Coherence
—

Weight: 3/25 of overall CLoS

Proxy for LTN 1/20 network density criterion (mesh width), using 1 of 3 criteria. Estimates how well each link connects to the wider cycling network based on nearby low-stress infrastructure density. Length-weighted average across route links.

LTN 1/20 CLoS Assessment Tool

This tool evaluates cycling routes in the West Midlands against the UK's LTN 1/20 Cycling Level of Service framework. It scores routes across five quality dimensions: safety, attractiveness, comfort, directness, and coherence.

📍

Set Origin & Destination

Click anywhere on the map to place your origin point (green), then click again to set the destination (red). The tool will find a stress-penalised route between them — preferring safer, higher-quality links.

📊

View CLoS Scores

After assessment, the route is coloured by link-level CLoS score (red = poor, green = good). The score card shows the overall CLoS percentage and a breakdown across all five LTN 1/20 dimensions. Tap the ⓘ icon next to each dimension to learn how it's calculated.

🗺️

Explore the Network

Use the display controls to colour the network by different metrics — overall CLoS, safety, attractiveness, junction assessment, or facility type. Toggle between forward and backward directions to see how scores differ by travel direction. Click any link on the map to inspect its properties.
